01

Identity

Lucid

A creator known for bots that linger. Characters built with care for nuance, atmosphere, and the weight of small moments. The work sits at the intersection of intimacy and imagination — romance, fantasy, and the tender space between.

Est. Character Creator · JanitorAI
02

Philosophy

Quality is the
only metric.

There is no rush. Each project is approached as a complete work — finished only when it feels inevitable. Presentation, personality, and detail are never afterthoughts. They are the work itself.

Patient · Deliberate · Uncompromising
03

Craft

Characters
that hold weight.

Romance. Fluff. Fantasy. Smut. The genre is secondary to the character. Every bot is written with enough interiority that the world it inhabits feels real. Feeling is the point.

Written · Felt · Released when ready
04

Presence

Design as
first impression.

Profiles are not decoration. They are the character's first breath — the moment a visitor decides whether to stay. Composition, typography, and atmosphere are considered with the same care as the writing itself.

Visual · Editorial · Immersive
05

Current

Always building.
Seldom rushing.

At any given moment there are characters being refined, worlds being sketched, and decisions being unmade. The process is the practice. Find the community — or the bots — when they arrive.
